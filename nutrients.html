Guide to Important Nutrients

Carbohydrates

Primary energy source — choose whole grains, legumes, fruit and vegetables for sustained energy.

  • Examples: Rice, whole wheat, oats, potatoes

Proteins

Needed for repair and growth — include lean meats, dairy, legumes and nuts.

  • Examples: Chicken, fish, eggs, lentils, paneer

Fats

Essential for brain health and hormone production. Prefer unsaturated fats.

  • Examples: Olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds, fatty fish

Fiber, Vitamins & Minerals

Fiber aids digestion; vitamins & minerals support immunity and metabolism.

  • Examples: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains, leafy greens
